About


Make a Cleaner Tomorrow is a organization committed to create a safer and cleaner future. Our mission is to educate the public on the societal and environmental effects of e-waste and make a difference in fighting climate change by providing free e-waste pickup. Many of these electronic products are poorly recycled and cause environmental problems. We aim to lessen these effects by properly recycling these devices to be reused for other products and to save natural resources.
